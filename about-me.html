About Me

Salutations Adventurers! I am the NPC GM! I have been known to be called, "Neil" from realm to realm. I have been enthralling my patrons for 12 years on many adventures. From submerged underground labyrinths to the far reaches of the galaxy, I have the power to guide you through it all. Yes, from the Pathfinder Society to the Starfinder Society my name has been etched into the history books as a "firm but fair" guide, a "detail oriented" master of ceremonies and a "flexible" story teller. If interested in having me as your guide through any adventure, whether in-person or online, please fill out your contact info on the magic form on my "Contact" page. Happy Adventuring!
